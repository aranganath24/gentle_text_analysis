EL DIARIO de laFebrero, 1976 Volume IV Boulder, AztlanEl Dario de la Gente is an Independent newspaper and magazine published by Chicano students at the University of Colorado at Boulder. We publish our newspaper monthly during the school year, with a magazine at the end of the semester. The editorial content does not necessarily reflect the views of any Chicano organizations on campus.
