Order restrains Rep. BenevidezA temporary restraining order in a lawsuit accusing State Rep. Betty Benevidez. D-Denver and her husband Waldo os assaulting a teen age girl was issued in Denver County Court Thursday. April 11. Mrs. Benevidez is accused of assaulting Arlene Garcia, 14. near the Benevidez home on March 12 and her husband is accused of preventing Karen Ruiz, 15. and Pat Montero, 16. from going to the girl's aid. County Judge Samuel M. Kirbens ordered the Benevidez's to refrain from "in any way harming, striking, molesting, or communicating" with the girls or their mothers, all of whom are plaintiffs in the civil suit.
