1974-75 academic yearWe want Chicanas y ChicanosWhen considering college and university education, the Chicano high school graduate or GED student should give the United Mexican-American Students and the Educational Opportunity Program top priority. UMAS-EOP supportive services are among the best in the nation, and can help the Chicano student get the most out of the University of Colorado educational experiences Our services include counseling, tutorial aid, academic advising, MexicanAmerican classes, writing and general English tutoring, financial aid assistance, work-study aid, concentrated services and even recreational activities. In addition, the University of Colorado is in itself one of the nations educational leaders. We have the best professors, most complete academic environment and the best overall educational program in the entire Southwestern United States.
