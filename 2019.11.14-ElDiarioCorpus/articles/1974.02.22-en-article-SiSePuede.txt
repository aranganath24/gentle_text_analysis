UMAS NEWS LETTERSi Se Puede!!Si, Se Puede! Our goal is to explain the purpose and interests of the United Mexican American Students (UMAS) organization. Thru sincere dedication and concern UMAS has grown to more than 1.200 students at the University of Colorado. Many have devoted time to various student activities which involve students of all races as well as Chicanos. It is our intent to establish effective communications among the people of our communities and the students of active organizations. We hope to encourage aspirations and direct responses from all.Ê
