Roybal appointed coordinator
Felipe Roybal filled the counselor coordinator position for UMAS-EOP temporarily on Sept. 25 when Ben Cordova resigned. Felipe was voted into the position at the staff meeting held Sept. 19, but said that he wouldn't take the position unless the counseling staff also wanted him in that position. The counselors affirmed his appointment as the counseling componant head. The job position will be open for Counseling Componant head at the end of October in agreement with Affirmative Action guidelines set down by the university. Felipe said that he is applying tor the position. Ben Cordova is now working for WICHE in Boulder.
