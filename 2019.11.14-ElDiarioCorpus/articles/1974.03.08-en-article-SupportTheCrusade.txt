Support the Crusade. There will be a rally to demonstrate support for the Crusade for Justice in the University Fountain Area, Wednesday, March 13 during the noon hour. The demonstration will feature Los Tres del Barrio of Los Angeles, Rodolfo “Corky” Gonzales, Ernest Vigil and “Bones” Rodrigues. The rally is being organized to show support for the March 17 day of commemoration set in Denver, the scene of the St. Patrick’s Day Massacre one year ago.
