Cultural Event to be held
A cultural event for the people of Aztlan, sponsored by the Comite Florcanto will be happening at the University of Albuquerque, Albuquerque High School, and Albuquerque Indian School, the week of August 3-6. The Indian/Chicano event will have gente from all over the southwest; writers, artists, organizers, teatros, and a Pow-wow on the last day. They need more involvement from groups throughout the southwest and
they need your participation. If you would like to know anything about what is going on or if you want to participate, write to Comite Florcanto c/o Teresa Marques, 2714 Rio Grande Blvd., Albuquerque, N.M., 87104. This is a cultural event that will echo throughout the southwest.
