Fellowship award
William Manzanares has been awarded a University of Colorado Graduate Fellowship for 1974-75. A graduate of Centennial High School, Manzanares received his BA in physics from Adams State College in 1967. After four years in the USAF he was appointed Director of Field Operations at the Juarez-Lincoln Center in Denver. He has been awarded $2500, plus tuition for studies leading to a Ph.D. in nuclear physics.
