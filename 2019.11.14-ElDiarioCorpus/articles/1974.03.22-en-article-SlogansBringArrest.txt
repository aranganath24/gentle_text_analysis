Slogans bring arrestÒThe Denver Police murdered Luis Martinez, remember March 17,Ó read the freshly painted slogans on doors throughout campus. Minutes later Judy Sandoval and Randy Esquibel, both students at the University of Colorado, were arrested by campus police and taken to Boulder County Jail. Both are charged with two fourth class felony counts, conspiracy and criminal mischief. If convicted, each count carries a maximum $120,000 fine and a two to twenty year sentence in the State Penitentiary. Following their arrest, Ms. Sandoval and Mr. Esquibel appeared before Judge Martin Steinberg requesting Personal Recognizance Bonds. After reviewing the charges, Judge Steinberg granted them bond at $ 1,000 each. Their preliminary hearings are Scheduled for March 18 at 11:00 am in Boulder County Court.
