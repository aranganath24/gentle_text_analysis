Ricardo Martinez coordinator of MAP

The position of Coordinator Ricardo V. Martinez of the Migrant Action Program is unique 
in that he must work not only with full time staff, but also with the student staff of the 
program. Martinez’ major responsibility is to coordinate the various components of the 
program with that of the University. In order to achieve this he must plan the 
development, growth, direction and budget of the program so that the needs of the 
students are anticipated and met. All efforts are made to have the student staff and 
student body involved in the decision making process of the Migrant Action Program.
