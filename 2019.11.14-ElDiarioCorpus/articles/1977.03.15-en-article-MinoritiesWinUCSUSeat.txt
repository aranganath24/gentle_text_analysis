Freddy Trujillo, an UMAS student running under the Minority Coalition ticket was elected to the University of Colorado Student Union (UCSU) Executive Council. Trujillo has been extremely active with UMAS functions in the past and possesses much experience in student government. Of the 17 candidates running for the five Executive Council spots, Trujillo gathered the third highest ballot total to assure him a seat. A referendum to allow student fees to be used on UCSU publications was defeated by a 1002-1785 count, in a light voter turnout.
