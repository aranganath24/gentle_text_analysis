Los Seis Live on Forever en espanol
Seven years ago two, yet unexplained, explosions, in Boulder, Colorado took the lives of six Chicanos: HERIBERTO TERAN, FLORENCIO GRANADO, FRANCISCO DOUGHERTY, NEVA ROMERO, UNA JAAKODA and REYES MARTINEZ. ANTONIO ALCANTAR was maimed. LOUIS “Jr.” MARTINEZ was killed in a 1973 police attack. RICARDO FALCON was assassinated in Nuevo Mexico in 1972. RITO CANALES and ANTONIO CORDOVAwere murdered by police in Nuevo Mexico. CARLOS ZAPATA died in the line of duty. MARIO VASQUEZ lives separated from his family and friends. RUBEN SALAZAR was murdered for writing/reporting the truth. FRANCISCO LUEVANO, CHUCO MONDRAGON, ANGIE LUEVANO, and SANDRA MAESTAS died tragic deaths after long years of service to the communities. Numerous brothers and sisters have given their lives while others have endured physical and psychological repression as part of this struggle by the oppressed working people against the existing order. To achieve the liberation of out people and control over the resources and industries of this country, we must not only commemorate the Symbols of Resistance but we must take up where they left off!!
