El Diario Note

Andres Figueroa Cordero, member of the Puerto Rican Nationalist Party and one of the five Puerto Rican Nationalists, who had been in prison for 20 years and who was recently released because he was terminally ill with cancer, died March 7, 1979 at 7:01 A.M. in Puerto Rico. Plans for a mass and funeral are pending. El Diario in a following issue will do a follow-up on the history of the Puerto Rican and the funeral proceedings in Puerto Rico.
