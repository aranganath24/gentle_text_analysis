Len Avila, former director of the Colorado Migrant Council, will be among four guests discussing the pros, cons and solutions to the plight of undocumented workers in the U.S.; June 23 at 7 p.m. on Channel 6 (Denver). Also participating will be Dave Cordova, visiting Law professor at the University of Colorado and the University of Denver, James Harden, Deputy District Director of the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service, and Sam Zahkem of the Colorado House of Representatives. Colorado Senator Floyd Haskell and Cristobal Alderte, co-chairperson of the Economic Development Commission, will also be interviewed.
