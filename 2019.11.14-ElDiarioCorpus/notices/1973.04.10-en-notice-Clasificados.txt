ClasificadosPor Nada El Diario is now able to offer free space for your classified ads, except those ads trying to sell something for profit. Any notices of services, organizations, events, personal messages, or other worthy causes will be published free on a space-available basis. All notices must be typed, double-spaced on a 60 space line, and must be no longer than 20 lines. El Diario reserves the right to omit, but not edit, any classifieds not in accordance with our policies of decency or fairness.
