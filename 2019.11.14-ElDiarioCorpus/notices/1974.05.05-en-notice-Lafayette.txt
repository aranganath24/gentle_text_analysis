LAFAYETTE
A Cinco de Mayo fiesta will be held at Centaurus High School on May 0, from 12 noon to 2:30 pm.. There will be music, dancing and speakers. Flo Saiz, teacher of La Cliicana class at Community College in Denver, and Ernest Vigil, principle of Encuela Tlatelolco will be speakers. Inmates from the Colorado State Penitentiary will also speak on Chicano experiences in prison. There will be no admission charge, but there will be a small fee for food.
