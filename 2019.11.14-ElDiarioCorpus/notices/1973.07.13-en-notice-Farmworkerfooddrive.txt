NOTICIA Farmworker food drive

UMAS and FLTF are conducting a food drive of al 1 nonperishable goods (no clothes) to help the UFW farmworkers in the San Luis Valley, who are on strike. All persons willing to donate food should bring it to the UMAS office, UMC Room 187, between 9:00 and 5:00, by Monday, July 16.
