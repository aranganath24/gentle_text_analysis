Bulletin
Leaders of the American Indian Movement and the Crusade for Justice have called for a demonstration in Denver today to show support and solidarity for our brothers at Wounded Knee, S.D. The demonstrators will leave the Crusade at 16th and Downing and march to the Federal Courthouse. The march will begin at 1 p.m. “We at the Crusade for Justice are calling for support of our hermanos at Wounded Knee in hopes that a blood bath at the hands of the Federal authorities, like those in the past, can be prevented.”
