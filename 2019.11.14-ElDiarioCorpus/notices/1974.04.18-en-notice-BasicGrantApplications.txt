Basic grant applications for the 1974-75 academic year are now available in the Financial Aid office. 2nd floor Administrative Annex. If you started post high school education after April 1, 1973 and will be a full-time student in 1974-75. you may be eligible for a grant as large as $8OO. After completing the application form and sending it to Washington. DC. you will be notified of your eligibility within four weeks. Then you should send the notification to the school you are interested in attending, and the amount of the Basic Grant will be calculated. If you have any questions about the grants, feel free to contact ,the Financial Aid Office at 443-221 1x6281
