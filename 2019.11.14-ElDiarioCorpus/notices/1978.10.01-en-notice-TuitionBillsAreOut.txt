TUITION BILLS ARE OUT! The Finance Office has mailed out its Fall semester tuition and fee bills. If you have not received a bill by October 5, you should contact the Finance Office in Regent Hall immediately to get a copy. If there are any financial aid errors on the bill or you are expecting a financial aid award, but have not received one, please bring the bill to the Financial Aid office, Willard 276 by the 12th of October. Late charges and disenrollment will occur after this date if the office of Financial Aid does not have a copy of the bill. The Office of Financial Aid will answer all questions concerning problems with fee bills and awards. Please come by Willard 267 before the 13th of October.
