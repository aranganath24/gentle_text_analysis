Meeting Scheduled for 17th

The Migrant Action Program will have a general meeting on October 17 at the 
Chemistry Room 132 at 7:00 p.m. according to Bill Samora, Program director. The 
purpose of this meeting is to serve as an outlet for all MAP students' problems or 
grievances. This meeting will also be an opportunity for all new students to acquaint 
themselves with the staff and counselors. On the agenda will be the Financial Aid 
Report concerning packets and tutorial services; Directors’ Reports and Individual Staff 
Reports (upon request) and the present day activities. All MAP students are urged to 
attend this important meeting.
