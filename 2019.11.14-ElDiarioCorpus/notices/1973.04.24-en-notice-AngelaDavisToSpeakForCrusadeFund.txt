Angela Davis to speak for Crusade fundBlack activist Angela Davis will be in Denver April 28 at 2:30 p.m. at the CPA Theater of Loretto Heights College. She is coming to Denver to show support for the defendants of the Crusade for Justice. Revenue generated by her appearance will be donated to the defense fund for those charged in the March 17 shootout with the Denver police. The incident left one Chicano, Luis Martinez, dead from police bullets. Admission to the Davis talk is: $2 per person, under 15, $1 and Children under 6, free.
