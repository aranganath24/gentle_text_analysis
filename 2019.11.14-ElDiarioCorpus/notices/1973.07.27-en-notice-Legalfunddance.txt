Legal fund dance

On Thursday, Aug. 2, a dance will be held at the G.A.O. building, 47th and Lipan in Denver, with all proceeds going to the Denver Chicano Legal Defense Committee. Donations of $3 for each couple will be asked, and music will be provided by Ernesto and the Good times. Tickets for the dance can be purchased at the UMAS office, UMC 187. Support your brothers and have a good time. Si se puede!
