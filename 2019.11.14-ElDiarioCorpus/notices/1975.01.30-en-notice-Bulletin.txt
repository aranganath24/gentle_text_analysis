Bulletin
There will be a statewide rally in support of Randy and Dora Esquibel and others arrested in La Junta. Rally will be in La Junta. Saturday Feb. I at 2:(W) P.M. The rally will consist of a march from City Park on 10th & Colorado to the Otero County Courthouse. Financial assistance for legal expenses can be sent in c/o El Comite Para Apoyar la Familia Esquibel y la Raza de La Junta.
