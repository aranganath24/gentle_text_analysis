New class offered

"Society and Education in the Southwest, Mexico and Latin America" is being offered for advanced undergraduate and graduate students. The class not previously advertised will meet Thursday evenings in Hellems Annex 138 throughout the spring semester and can be added immediately. Historical and present realities of the American Southwest, Mexico and Latin America is the main emphasis of the course. The course is part of an overall

program which includes Spanish language training and professional courses in Colima. Mexico in the summer of 1974 through the University of Oklahoma. Those interested in the summer program must talk to Dr. Kraft specially at extension 6122 or 494-0696. Regularly enrolled students for the spring semester will be charged $l5 enrollment fee. Special students will be charged $ 22.50 per credit hour with a maximum of $ 67.50 for the 3 hour course only.
