W.I.E. (Washington Internships in Education), a Ford Foundation sponsored project is seeking applications. In this program the intern has the opportunity to increase skills and knowledge_in educational policy making areas; to learn which issues are of vital concern at these levels, how agencies relate to their constituencies, and how interaction between different branches of government and the private sector combine to make educational policy. W.I.C. is particularly anxious to increase the number of Chicanos in this program. Interested parties call Sal Ramirez ext. 8852 for appointment.
