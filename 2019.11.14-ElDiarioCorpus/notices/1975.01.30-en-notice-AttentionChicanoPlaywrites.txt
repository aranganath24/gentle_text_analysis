! Attention Chicano Play writes! The Teatro Chicano of El Paso Community College (EPCC), under the auspices of the Expansion Arts Program is promoting a Teatro Chicano Playwriting Contest. The winning contestant will be awarded a prize of $2OO and his play will be performed in different locations by the Teatro Chicano Troupe of EPCC. The script should in some way be a manifestation of Chicano culture or a reflection of the bicultural milieu. Contest rules and entry form can be obtained by writing to: Teatro Chicano, EPCC, 6601 Dyer, El Paso, Texas 79904. All entries must be received by April 15, 1975.
