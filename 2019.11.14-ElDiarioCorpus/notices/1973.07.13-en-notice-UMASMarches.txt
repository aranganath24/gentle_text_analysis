BULLETIN! UMAS MARCHES

The UMAS organization has called for a student, staff, and faculty march in protest of University hiring practices. Students will assemble in the UMC fountain area at 1030 a-m. Monday and march to the Recreation building. UMAS is calling for an investigation into University hiring practices and for the resignation of William S. Appenzeller, Recreation Director for the University. The entire University community is asked to participate in the march.
