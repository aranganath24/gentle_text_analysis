Psychology majors Professor A1 Ramirez will give an informal talk dealing with careers in psychology for Chicano psychology students. The talk and discussion will be held at noon on April 26, Thursday, in room 156 of the Muenzinger (Psych.) Bldg. All Chicano psych, majors or interested in majoring in psych, are urged to attend the meeting. Bring your own lunch! For further information contact Dr. Ramirez at ext. 8724.
