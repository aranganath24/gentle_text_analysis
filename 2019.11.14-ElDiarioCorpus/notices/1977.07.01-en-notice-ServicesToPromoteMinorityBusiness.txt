Services to promote minority business
Two documents to aid in promoting minority business enterprise are available from the Denver Federal Executive Boards minority business opportunity committee. The documents are a directory of minority owned business and a marketing guide listing government and industrial purchasers of goods
and services provided by minority-owned businesses. Both may be obtained free by contacting Dave Fresquez, MBOC executive director, 837-2152 or 837-3686, or by writing Fresquez at MBOC, 1405 Curtis St., Denver, Co. 80203.
