Watch Out! We’re the Secret Services

The third floor of the University Memorial Center holds some of the best kept secrets in the university, the Ombudsman Office is there to help you with a variety of problems. Our specialty is mediation in grievance situations. We also serve as an information and referral service for the campus and more generally, the Boulder community. If you have any difficulties - from Drop/Add hassles to appealing a grade - come see us! If you don’t have a problem but would like to know more about the campus community, student life, or if you just want to talk, our student and professional staff welcome you to visit us in the UMC, room 328. Our phone number is 492-5077. We hope we can help make your educational experience at the University of Colorado, Boulder, a pleasant one! We are the University’s answer to a one stop service center!
