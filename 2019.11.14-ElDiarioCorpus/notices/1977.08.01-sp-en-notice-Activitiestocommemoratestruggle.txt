Activities to commemorate struggleAn afternoon of activities in commemoration of the fight for decent wages, living conditions, education and the right to be free from oppression and racism has been set. It will be held August 27, 1977 from 12 noon - 4:00 p.m., at Our lady of Guadalupe Hall: W. 36th Ave. & Lipan, Denver, Colorado. For more information call 572-9414. The event is sponsored by Colorado Coalition in Support of the Chicano Liberation Struggle: Su Teatro, Caucus for Student Democratic Rights, Teatro De Causa DeLos Pobres, Community Legal Workers and the Colorado Organization for Revolutionary Struggle (MLM).
