A West African art exhibit will be held at the University of Colorado in the UMC Fine Arts Gallery March Ist through 19th. The Human Relations Center is presenting the exhibit in hopes of bringing to the community a visual experience of the variety of contemporary West African art. The free exhibit will be open to the publ'9 to 9 Monday through Thursday, 9 to 5 p.m. Friday and Saturday, and noon to 9 p.m. Sunday. For further information call 492-5077.
