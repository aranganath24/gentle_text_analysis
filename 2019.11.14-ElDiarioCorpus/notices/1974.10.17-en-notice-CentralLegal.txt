Central Legal is an organization of Chicano Law Students at the University of Colorado under the auspices of the Legal Aid and Defender Program. It offers legal assistance in both criminal and civil cases. We will be handling cases in the Lafayette and Longmont communities the following dates and places: Lafayette Centro Communidad 315 E.
