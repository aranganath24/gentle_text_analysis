UMAS-EOP is continuing to accept applications for fall entry into the University of Boulder campus. The tentative deadline for applications is August 1, 1973. All interested Mexican American and other minority students are encouraged to send in applications and other materials, such as ACT and SAT test scores, as soon as possible. For any other questions or assistance regarding admissions to CU, contact Tom Trujillo, assistant director of admissions, at 443-2211, ext. 7555.
