WHAT? Not Another Election? Yes, folks, it’s election time again! Due to the new constitution, a new form of legislative body is to be elected, the Representative Council and Local Council. The election, to be held April 23-24 (Primary) April 30-May 1 (Finals) will select 10 at-large representatives and 1 representative from Arts & Sciences for Local Council.
