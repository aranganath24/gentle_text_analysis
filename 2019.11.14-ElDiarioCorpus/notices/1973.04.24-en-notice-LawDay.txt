Law Day The keynote speaker for Law Day 1973 presented Tuesday May 1st by the Student Bar Association of the University of Colorado will be Mr. Charles Morgan Jr. who is currently in the national spotlight as the Democratic attorney for the Watergate Affair. He is presently serving as Director of the Southern Regional Office of the American Civil Liberties Union in Atlanta, Georgia, and is actively involved in civil liberties and civil rights litigation. He will speak at noon in the Lindsley Memorial Courtroom of Fleming Law Building and in Macky Auditorium at either 4 p.m. or 4:30 p.m.
