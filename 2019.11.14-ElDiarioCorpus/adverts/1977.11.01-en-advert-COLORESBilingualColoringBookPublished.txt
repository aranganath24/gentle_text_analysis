COLORES Bilingual Coloring Book Published The drawing below was taken from Colores, a bilingual, bicultural coloring book designed by Denver artist A1 Sanchez. Colores is designed as an educational tool for children 3 - 8 years old with simple words in both Espanol and Ingles. In addition to coloring pages, the book features dolls and cutouts. The book is the first of a series of educational publications planned by Sanchez. It would make an excellent gift for any Chicanito/a. Copies are available for $1.25 each from A1 Sanchez, 1035 S.
