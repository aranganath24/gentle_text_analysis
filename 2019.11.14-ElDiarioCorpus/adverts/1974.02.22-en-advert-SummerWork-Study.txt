Summer work- studySummer Work Study Students wishing to work for the University during the summer must apply by April l, 1974. Applications may be obtained at the Student Employment Services. Administrative Annex (Willard Hall) Room 180-181. Any students interested in working for the Summer UMASEOP as a tutor, counselor or teaching assistant must apply for summer work study. The program is designed to give continuing students employment opportunities and a chance to save money for the next academic year.
