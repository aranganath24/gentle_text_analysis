NCHO Are you considering a career in Medicine? Psychiatry? Dentistry? Wherever your interest lies, NCHO (National Chicano Health Organization) can provide you with the opportunity to find out more about medical field. We are an organization composed of pre-health major students in order to help each other. We have started an exam/notes for all science classes. This is not all of our services to say the least! Throughout the year NCHO will provide health related projects, such as established blood pressure screening stand and physical exam clinic.
