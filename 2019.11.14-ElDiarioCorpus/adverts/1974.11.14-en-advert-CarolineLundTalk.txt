SPEAKING ON: Caroline Lund speaking on women in the Soviet Union and China Ms. Lund is a 29-year-old Journalist who has written extensively for the MILITANT newspaper. She spent 1969-70 in Europe and in 1973 traveled to France to report on current developments there. She recently returned from Tokyo where she covered the Asian Youth Conference at which women's liberation was a central topic of discussion. THURS.
