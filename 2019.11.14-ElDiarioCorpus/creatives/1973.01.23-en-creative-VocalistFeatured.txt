vocalist featured
Rita Dolores Orona, a music major at CU, has been selected soloist singer for Puccini’s opera “Mi Chiamano Mimi" which will be on the Honor's Concert program for January 26. The concert will be at Macky Auditorium at 8 p.m. Friday, and no admission fee will be charged.
Rita was selected from among all the students, including undergraduates, graduates and masters, in the CU School of Music. She will be auditioning for a possible position with the Metropolitan Opera of New York on the day of the concert.
