CAMA Y TORTILLAS Levan tate Chicana Desprendete de la cama Comprate las tortillas, Tampoco tu vives de rodillas o de espaldas o nalgas. La Huelga te requiere. La Lucha te reclama. La Causate suplica. La educacion te llama. Despierta ahora Chicana Escoge tu revolucion Sea educar por ideologia o tomar parte de la accion. Awake and arise Chicana The Revolution has said You can’t win lying down prostrated in a bed or making tortillas forever which could be bought instead. Stand up and leave behind, A life spent on your knees. The Huelga needs you. La Causa implores you, Education is calling you. Choose your own revolution of action or ideology For in educating yourself. You’ll educate the whole family. M.T. Gaitan
